WHY US?

  • Our furniture fronts are sourced from British suppliers, and carcasses built by a local British cabinet maker. Most branded kitchens have the added expense of marketing and haulage across Europe/Asia included in their price.
  • All cabinets are rigid carcasses manufactured with high quality 18mm thick MFC, including the back panel. Always push the back wall of a kitchen cabinet to check the stability. Telltale signs such as this determine thier longevity. We don't compromise on material costs.
  • We pride ourselves in providing a wide range of melamine faced carcasses available for no extra charge. You won't be stuck with a vulnerable white interior to your stunning new kitchen, just to save on material costs.
  • Our drawer boxes are award winning Hettich ArciTech boxes, which come as standard. Typically a drawer box of this quality is an optional extra totalling hundreds of pounds.
  • The kitchens we pride ourselves in delivering are built to last. No hidden costs. Pure and simple.
