Who are we?

Based in Marlow, Buckinghamshire, Planitt was created by Chris, an experienced Kitchen Designer, who spent the first part of his career working for a high-end kitchen showroom. Having developed his skills during numerous projects of all different shapes and sizes over the years, the desire to provide customers with a personal design service and high quality product, at a more affordable price, was born; and so was Planitt...

Planning a new kitchen can be a daunting task, which is why we offer a bespoke design service, tailored to each customer's specific requirements and, through the use of excellent photorealistic renders, can show you exactly what your new space will look like, giving you the peace of mind that you are making the right choices before going through with the purchase of your new kitchen. There are endless furniture styles and colours to choose from, which have all been carefully selected due to their quality and durability, enabling us to give you a showroom-quality product but at a much more competitive price.

As well as providing a precise kitchen design & supply service, Planitt is also available should you simply want a new space visualising. So whether you are starting the process of exploring a new kitchen, have a design already in mind and simply want an excellent product to match your budget or have a home renovation project which you are struggling to picture in your mind, we look forward to hearing from you.
